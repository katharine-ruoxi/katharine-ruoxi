Online Retail Mastery & Offline Insights

My journey in retail is a tale of two worlds: the dynamic, fast-paced realm of online retail, and the tangible, sensory-rich experience of offline stores. This unique upbringing gave me an early appreciation for the offline retail experience, setting the stage for my future career.

D2C E-commerce Expertise

This experience has been instrumental in understanding the end-to-end process of retail in the digital age.

Early Gaming Experiences & Discovering Otome Games

Initially, video games barely piqued my interest, except for a few notable exceptions during my youth, such as "Monopoly 4," various car racing titles, and "Delta Force." This tepid enthusiasm extended to a day-long engagement with "Civilization." My exploration spanned a range of games - from "Dota" and "The Legend of Sword and Fairy" to "World of Warcraft" and "The Legend of Zelda." Despite this, I never considered myself a gaming aficionado. That changed dramatically when a friend introduced me to otome games. Despite initial skepticism, I found myself deeply engrossed in this genre, leading to a significant realization: my lack of interest in gaming stemmed not from a general disinterest but from not having encountered games designed with my preferences in mind.

Understanding the Gaming Industry

This journey ignited a curiosity about the gaming industry, particularly the oversight of female gamers in a field predominantly designed by and for males. My immersion into the gaming world allowed me to quickly grasp the industry's nuances and identify my aspirations within it: to deliver well-designed gaming experiences that provide value and support to players, especially those often overlooked by mainstream gaming narratives.

Vision for Female-Centric Gaming

Motivated by the desire to create inclusive and emotionally supportive gaming experiences, I am now focused on developing titles that cater specifically to female gamers and other underserved demographics. My aim is to design games that offer more than entertainmentâ€”they offer companionship, guidance, and mentorship, enhancing players' lives with emotional depth and engagement.

Current Endeavors & Future Ambitions

I am currently planning to develop a variety of games, including casual and simulation titles, with a particular focus on female-oriented narratives. This initiative is not just about creating games; it's about acknowledging the diverse spectrum of gamers and providing experiences that resonate with a broader audience.

Conclusion

From casual gaming to a profound appreciation for otome genres, my gaming journey reflects a deep-seated belief in the transformative power of inclusive and thoughtful game design. As I venture further into this field, my goal is to contribute to a more diverse and emotionally enriching gaming landscape, one that welcomes and celebrates the varied experiences of all players.
